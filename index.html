Product

NIRFI™

A palm-sized NIR fluorescence observation device for teams that need a lighter, easier-to-deploy workflow than conventional cart-based systems.

Weight ~0.5 kg (device only) / ~1.5 kg (with case)
Connectivity USB (3 m cable, extendable to ~5 m)
Storage Direct PC save — no external recorder needed
Mounting Standard tripod compatible
