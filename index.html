Why It Matters

Three reasons the format is easier to evaluate and deploy.

01

Fits into existing workspaces

The palm-sized body and USB power help teams set up fluorescence imaging without dedicating a large footprint to the device.

02

Capture stays on the host PC

Images are saved directly to the connected computer, which keeps review, export, and documentation in the same workflow.

03

Portable enough for shared use

Approximate device weight and tripod compatibility make it easier to move between benches, demos, and evaluation environments.
