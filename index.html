Product

NIRFI™

A compact, easy-to-use NIR fluorescence observation device for research and professional use. Designed for teams that need real-time fluorescence visualization without the overhead of conventional systems.

Weight ~0.5 kg (device only) / ~1.5 kg (with case)
Connectivity USB (3 m cable, extendable to ~5 m)
Storage Direct save to host PC for review, export, and documentation
Mounting Standard tripod compatible
