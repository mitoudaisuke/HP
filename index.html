Product

NIRFI™

A compact NIR fluorescence observation device for research, training, and professional use. Designed for teams that need real-time fluorescence visualization and direct PC-based capture in a smaller, simpler format.

Weight ~0.5 kg (device only) / ~1.5 kg (with case)
Connectivity USB (3 m cable, extendable to ~5 m)
Storage Direct save to host PC for review, export, and documentation
Mounting Standard tripod compatible
