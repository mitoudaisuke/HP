Near-Infrared Fluorescence Imaging

Compact NIR fluorescence imaging, direct to PC.

NIRFI™ is a palm-sized near-infrared fluorescence imaging system for research, training, and professional evaluation workflows. Connect by USB, observe in real time, and save images directly to a host PC.
