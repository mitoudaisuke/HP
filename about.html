About

Toa Optics Co., Ltd.

Toa Optics is a Tokyo-based company focused on compact near-infrared fluorescence imaging for research, training, and professional evaluation.

Mission

Making near-infrared fluorescence imaging more accessible for research, training, and professional evaluation.

We focus on device formats and workflows that are easier to review, set up, and carry into practical working environments. NIRFI™ is our current product expression of that approach.
