About Us

Toa Optics Co., Ltd.

A Tokyo-based deep-tech startup dedicated to making near-infrared fluorescence imaging accessible to clinicians and researchers worldwide.

Our Mission

Democratizing NIR Imaging

Near-infrared fluorescence imaging has long been confined to large hospital systems with the budget for bulky, expensive equipment. Toa Optics exists to change that.

We believe that a surgeon in a regional hospital, a researcher in a university lab, or a clinician in a developing country deserves the same imaging capabilities as the world's leading medical centers.

NIRFI™ is our answer: a palm-sized, USB-powered NIR fluorescence imager that makes precision tissue visualization affordable, portable, and genuinely simple to use.
