About

Toa Optics Co., Ltd.

Toa Optics is a Tokyo-based company focused on compact near-infrared fluorescence imaging. We build products and workflows intended for research, training, and professional evaluation.

Mission

Make fluorescence imaging easier to evaluate and easier to bring into real working environments.

Our current focus is compact hardware, direct PC-based capture, and lower-overhead setup for teams that do not want unnecessary complexity in early evaluation, training, or workflow review.

NIRFI™ is the clearest expression of that focus: a portable near-infrared fluorescence imaging system designed around compact size, USB power, and straightforward image capture.
