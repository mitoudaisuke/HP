About Us

Toa Optics Co., Ltd.

Toa Optics is a Tokyo-based company focused on compact near-infrared fluorescence imaging. We design and develop imaging tools that make fluorescence observation and image capture simpler to deploy in research, training, and professional settings.

What We Do

Focused on practical fluorescence imaging

Our core product, NIRFI™, is a portable near-infrared fluorescence observation device designed for real-time visualization and direct PC-based image capture.

We focus on compact hardware, simple USB-based setup, and lower-overhead workflows than conventional fluorescence imaging systems.

Why Work With Us

Built for practical evaluation and deployment

Focused Product Scope

We are not presenting a broad catalog of unrelated technologies. Our current focus is compact NIR fluorescence imaging.

Portable System Design

NIRFI™ is designed around portability, direct PC connectivity, and straightforward operation for teams that need a simpler imaging workflow.

Global Market Orientation

We are based in Tokyo and actively engaging with international partners, exhibitions, and business development programs.

Partnership Readiness

We welcome discussions with distributors, research partners, and organizations evaluating fluorescence imaging workflows.
