Privacy

Privacy summary

This page describes the current handling summary for inquiry information submitted through this site.

Information we collect

We may collect the information you submit through our contact form, including name, email address, organization, country or region, intended use, and message content.

Purpose of use

We use this information only to respond to your inquiry and related communication about products, demonstrations, pricing, partnership, and support topics connected to your request.

Third-party sharing

We do not provide inquiry information to third parties except where required to operate the inquiry process, comply with law, or protect our legitimate rights.

Service providers

We may use external service providers to process inquiries and host website infrastructure. These providers are used only to support the operation of the service.

Retention and management

Inquiry information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to answer the inquiry, continue related communication, or satisfy operational and legal requirements. We apply reasonable administrative and technical controls to manage the information.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the contact page and indicate that your inquiry concerns privacy or data handling.
