Product

NIRFI™

NIRFI™ is a compact near-infrared fluorescence imaging system for research, training, and professional evaluation workflows. Connect by USB, observe in real time, and save images directly to a host PC.

  • Approx. 0.5 kg
  • USB bus powered
  • Direct PC capture
  • Tripod compatible
