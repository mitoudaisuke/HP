Near-Infrared Fluorescence Observation

NIRFI™

NIRFI™ is a palm-sized, USB-powered near-infrared fluorescence observation device for research, training, and professional evaluation workflows. Capture real-time fluorescence images directly on a host PC.

  • Approx. 0.5 kg
  • USB bus powered
  • Direct PC capture
  • Tripod compatible

Key Benefits

Practical value for teams evaluating workflow fit.

Simple setup

USB connection and host-PC display keep setup compact and direct.

Portable deployment

Small enough for bench-top evaluation, demonstrations, and shared workspaces.

Direct documentation

Save images directly to PC for review, reporting, and internal comparison.

How It Works

Three steps from connection to capture.

1

Connect

Connect NIRFI™ to the host PC with the included USB cable.

2

Position

Set the device by hand or on a standard tripod depending on the scene.

3

Observe & Capture

Observe near-infrared fluorescence in real time and save images directly to the PC.
